E-commerce

Overview

Our e-commerce website offers a convenient and secure online shopping experience. Customers can explore a wide range of products, from clothing and electronics to home goods. With an easy-to-use interface, you can quickly find and purchase your favorite items. Additionally, users can register to sell their products, reaching a larger audience.

Tool

JQuery
October CMS
MySql

Information

  • Architecture: MVC (Model-View-Controller).
  • Data Management with DataTables for interactive table displays and Select2 for enhanced dropdown selections.
  • Authorization and Authentication.
  • CMS: Built with October CMS, leveraging its powerful plugins for easy customization and scalability.
